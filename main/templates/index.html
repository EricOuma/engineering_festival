About Festival

Welcome to the Engineering Festival 2020

Engineering Festival is an annual conference that aims to empower engineering students in Africa with the objective of building engineering capacities, skills and innovation through speakers, panels, discussions, workshops and exhibitions. The conference is designed to enable engineering students in Africa to apply engineering skills and technology in addressing the needs in Africa and their immediate environment.
